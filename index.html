College Scorecard Viewer

A basic, pure React page that pulls data from the US Department of Educations College Scorecard API. Search for a school on the left, click it and navigate through the information on the main view. I made this so I could get some experience working in react without a full stack framework. You will need a free API key to use it.

To Space

To Space is a quick and dirty Javascript game that displays to an HTML canvas element. It's played entirely with a mouse. Just click on the screen in the direction you want the rocket to move and try to get as high as you can. There are fuel drops along the way to help you go farther. Although simple and crude, all of the images are original and were made in Inkscape as SVG files.
